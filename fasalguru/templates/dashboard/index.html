Our Idea

The problem statement was an open-ended challenge aimed at showcasing our AI/ML skills by solving a real world problem.

We chose to address one of the most impactful domains agriculture by building an intelligent crop recommendation system for farmers. Our solution leverages machine learning to analyze factors such as soil parameters, climate conditions, and environmental data to suggest the most suitable crops for a given region or field.
To ensure accessibility and practical usability, we developed both a web-based platform and a desktop application, enabling farmers and stakeholders to easily interact with the system across different environments.

Web Service

Run AI/ML farming optimization models through a cloud-hosted web interface that supports real-time inference on soil and environmental inputs. The service delivers fast predictions, scalable access, and seamless integration for data-driven crop selection and farm decision-making.

Desktop Application

A standalone desktop application that enables local execution of AI/ML farming optimization models for fast, offline-capable inference. Provides a streamlined interface for entering soil and climate parameters and generating accurate crop and yield predictions with low latency.
